Dining at Shangri-La

The Beach House

Experience the epitome of culinary excellence at The Beach House. Our signature restaurant offers breathtaking panoramic ocean views, creating a romantic and sophisticated ambiance.

The Sandbar

For a more casual and laid-back dining experience, head to The Sandbar. Situated right on the beach, this vibrant grill offers a relaxed atmosphere where you can enjoy delicious food with your toes in the sand.

Poolside Cafe

Stay refreshed and energized without leaving the poolside at The Poolside Cafe. This convenient and casual spot offers a variety of light bites, including salads, wraps, and snacks, as well as a wide selection of refreshing beverages.
